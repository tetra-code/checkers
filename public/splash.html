How to play checkers

1. The goal is to jump the opponent's piece and remove them from the board.
2. You can only move diagonally forward, with the exception of the king.
3. When jumping an opponent's piece, you can do so backwards.
4. You can't jump more than once in a turn.
5. If a piece has the opportunity to jump, it must take the jump.
6. If a piece reaches the other end of the board, it becomes a king and it can move diagonally backwards.
7. To win you have to take all of the opponent's pieces.
8. If neither players can move, it is a draw.
